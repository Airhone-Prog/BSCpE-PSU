About This Portal
AutoSched — PSU CpE Department
What is AutoSched?

AutoSched is the automated scheduling system of the BS Computer Engineering Department at Pampanga State University. It generates conflict-free class and exam schedules for all sections, assigns teachers and rooms, and makes the final output available here — no login needed.

This web portal is the student- and faculty-facing viewer built on top of the system. Schedules are exported from the scheduling engine and displayed here for easy access on any device.

How to Use This Portal
🎒
For Students
Go to Schedules, choose your semester, year level, and section, then pick Class or Exam schedule. Hit View Schedule to see your timetable.
👨‍🏫
For Teachers
Go to Schedules, switch to Teacher tab, choose your semester and name, then pick your schedule type. Download your timetable as an image to save or share.
📅
Class Schedule
Shows the weekly grid of all your subjects — time, room, and teacher — organized by day of week.
📝
Exam Schedule
Shows Midterm or Final exam dates, times, and rooms for all your subjects. Filter by exam type using the toggle buttons.

RoleAccess LevelCapabilities

Student / Teacher View Only Browse and download schedules — no login required
Technical Details

AutoSched is a desktop application written in Python with a Tkinter GUI, backed by a portable self-contained MySQL instance. The scheduling engine uses constraint-based algorithms to eliminate teacher, room, and section conflicts automatically.

This viewer reads pre-exported JSON data files generated by the scheduling engine. No server or database connection is required to browse schedules.
